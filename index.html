voipdrupal by leoburd

VoIP Drupal is a framework that integrates Drupal sites with Internet-based telephony enabling the creation of a variety of services such as audio announcements, audio groups, audio events, audio polls, and many more.

Dependencies

The current version of Voip Drupal is dependent on Asterisk 1.6, Drupal 6 and Festival.
